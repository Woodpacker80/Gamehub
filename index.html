📋 How to Play
Wheel of Words
🎯 Objective
🏆Guess the hidden puzzle and earn as much money as possible. The player with the most money at the end wins!
🔄 How a Turn Works
1️⃣Press 🎰 SPIN to spin the wheel and land on a dollar value.
2️⃣After spinning, choose a consonant. Each time it appears in the puzzle you earn that dollar amount.
3️⃣A correct guess lets you spin again or buy a vowel. A wrong guess passes the turn.
🔤 Buying Vowels
💵Buying a vowel (A, E, I, O, U) costs $250. Vowels don't earn money but help crack the puzzle.
💡You can buy a vowel after spinning — as long as you have at least $250 and vowels remain.
✅ Solving the Puzzle
🧠Think you know it? Press ✓ SOLVE and type the full answer. Correct = you win the round! Wrong = opponent's turn.
⌨️Type plain letters only — no accents or diacritics. Type E instead of É.
⚠️ Special Wheel Segments
💸BANKRUPT — Lose all round earnings. Turn passes to opponent.
😬LOSE — Lose your turn. Round earnings stay safe.
🔻MINUS — Lose 50% of your round earnings. Ouch!
★BONUS — Land on it and guess a consonant. You earn the bonus value for every time the letter appears! ★ Silver: $2,500 — ★ Gold: $5,000 per letter.
★JOKER — A random letter is revealed. You earn $1,250 per time it appears! Your turn continues — spin again, buy a vowel or solve!
★ Silver Bonus Round
★In later rounds a silver BONUS tile appears on the wheel. Land on it and guess a consonant — you earn $2,500 for every time the letter appears!
🔻Silver rounds also have a Minus tile — landing on it costs you 50% of your round earnings!
★ Gold Bonus Round
★The final rounds go Gold! A gold BONUS tile ($5,000 per letter) and a silver BONUS tile ($2,500 per letter) are both on the wheel — two chances for a massive payout!
💰Vowels cost $500 in Gold rounds. Two ★ JOKER tiles and one Minus tile are also on the wheel!
⚡ Guess First!
⚡If no consonants remain and neither player can afford a vowel, Guess First kicks in! Remaining vowels are revealed one by one — be the first to solve it!
⏱️You have a few seconds after each reveal to press ✓ SOLVE before the next vowel drops.
👥 Play Against Each Other
📱Want to challenge a friend? Choose Play Against Each Other on the title screen or in the menu.
🔑Create a room and share the 4-letter code with your opponent. The game starts automatically when they join.
🪙At the start of each round a digital coin flip decides who goes first — fair and fast!
🎮You take turns. Your name is always on the left, your opponent on the right. The rules are exactly the same.
★The colored stars are your guide: ★ blue = JOKER  ·  ★ silver = Silver Bonus Round  ·  ★ gold = Gold Bonus Round.
🎮 Rounds & Winning
🔁Choose 3, 5, or 7 rounds before the game starts.
🏅After all rounds, the player with the highest total wins!
⌨️ Pro Tip
💻On desktop you can use your physical keyboard to guess letters — no clicking required!
